WHAT IS ARCIUM ABOUT?

  • ArciumHQ is pioneering the future of privacy with the world’s first parallelized confidential computing network. Built on Solana, it’s an encrypted supercomputer that processes data without ever exposing it—think finance, healthcare, and AI, but ful,ly secure.

  • Using advanced cryptography like Multi-Party Computation (MPC), Arcium lets developers and apps harness trustless, verifiable computing power. Backed by over $10M from big names like Coinbase Ventures and LongHash, it’s redefining how we handle sensitive data.

  • Their recent moves—like acquiring Inpher’s tech and team, launching a public testnet soon, and a token sale on CoinList—show they’re accelerating toward a mainnet launch. Arcium’s vision? A decentralized internet where privacy and security are non-negotiable.
